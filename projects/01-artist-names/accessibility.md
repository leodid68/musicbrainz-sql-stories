# Accessibility text

## Plotly chart

Horizontal bar chart of the ten most duplicated exact primary artist names in
the MusicBrainz export dated 2026-07-15. Indigo ranks first with 249 artist
entities, followed by Legion with 109. Merlin and Vortex each have 87. The
ranking excludes aliases, `[unknown]`, and `[no artist]`.

## PDF carousel

Five-page document explaining that artist names are not identifiers. It
introduces the MusicBrainz dataset, distinguishes 126,415 duplicated exact
names from the 419,770 artist entities they cover, shows the top-ten ranking,
explains the `GROUP BY`, `HAVING`, CTE, `COUNT(*)`, and `SUM()` logic, and ends
with the methodological limitations and the next analysis question.
